About Us

Kokari Agro-Environmental Services Ltd is an experienced and professional firm providing efficient and comprehensive services in the agricultural, horticultural and environmental sectors. 

We are committed to ensuring sustainable and economically viable project management with emphasis on Corporate Social Responsibility and
Environmental Conservation. Established in 2009, Kokari AgroEnvironmental Services Ltd is a specialist agribusiness development
company based in Sokoto state Operations are organized into three divisions, namely, Agro-Processing Services, Agribusiness Consulting Services and Landscape Services. We have implemented a diverse number of high impact strategic projects for various clients in Nigeria with in-depth expertise in the agricultural sector value chains and agro-clusters development We provide agricultural advisory services soft commodities sourcing/trading and processing to increase productivity our customers and clients and connect them to International markets while meeting critical compliance requirements.

Whether you are an individual or business in need of agricultural input or produce supply, Landscaping services or products, importer looking for good quality commodity; a supermarket in need of fresh produce every day; an agro-processor in need of agro processing materials; or even a relief organization in need of nutritious relief commodities, we will get you sorted! To sustain our complex sourcing efficiency, we diligently work with a network of farmers and producer groups to ensure that they increase heir production, comply with international regulations and
standards while remaining socioeconomically sustainable.
